About the project

Fekra plus An intelligent platform that evaluates the feasibility of business ideas using AI-powered analysis. Users can input details about their project — such as budget, target market, and expected revenue — and the system gathers real market data from trusted sources like Google Trends, Statista, and Amazon. It then analyzes this data to generate a visual report, including profitability insights, market trends, recommendations, and an estimated success probability all in one place.
